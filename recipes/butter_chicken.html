Butter Chicken Recipe

Home


Begin by marinating the chicken. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Add boneless chicken pieces and let them marinate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.

Grill or pan-fry the marinated chicken until lightly charred and cooked through. Set aside. In a separate pan, melt butter and sauté onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato purée, and spices like garam masala, cumin, and coriander. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.

Add cream and a splash of water to adjust consistency. Stir in the cooked chicken and simmer for 10–15 minutes until the flavors meld. Finish with a pinch of kasuri methi (dried fenugreek) and a swirl of cream.

Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed basmati rice. Garnish with fresh coriander and a drizzle of butter.

Marinade

  • 500g boneless chicken (thigh or breast)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Gravy

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 cup tomato purée
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi
  • Salt to taste

Serving

  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Extra butter or cream (optional)
  • Naan, roti, or rice
